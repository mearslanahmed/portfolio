## Overview

Kaffeine replaces traditional counter ordering with a seamless digital menu and in-app order flow tailored specifically for small coffee shops and local businesses.

## Problem

Small businesses often rely on chaotic counter ordering and lack the technical team to run and maintain expensive enterprise POS or ordering systems.

## Solution

A lightweight, robust Android application that handles the entire ordering lifecycle with zero necessary on-site technical infrastructure.

## Features & Architecture

- **Clean Architecture**: Built with UI, logic, and data layers fully separated. Adding a new feature doesn't require touching the entire codebase.
- **Serverless Backend**: Utilizes Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore for real-time inventory and order syncing without maintaining a custom server.
- **Native Android UI**: Leverages RecyclerView and Material Design for a highly responsive, native feel.
